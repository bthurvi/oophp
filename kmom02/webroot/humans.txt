Urbax is a PHP-bolierplate/framework for small websites.
Urbax was built by Urban Vikdahl as a part of a web programming course
I read autum 2015.

It was based on the design principles  by Mikael Roos 
(me@mikaelroos.se) as part of teaching web programming 
and published in june 2013 as an article at http://dbwebb.se/kunskap (swedish).
